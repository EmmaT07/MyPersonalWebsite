Our mission at Sunstone is to enhance the beauty and functionality of homes and commercial spaces with premium quality quartz products. We offer a diverse range of designs to meet the unique tastes of our customers. ABOUT SUNSTONE

Established in 2019, Sunstone International is a leading manufacturer of premium quality quartz products. Our facility is located in Bangladesh and we specializes in high-quality quartz products known for their durability, versatility, and low maintenance. Our offerings include full slab wholesale and professional fabrication services. Our R&D team is dedicated to creating and customizing unique designs, from contemporary and modern to traditional and rustic, inspired by the natural beauty and unique patterns of quartz. At Sunstone, we are committed to providing exceptional product quality, design options, and customer service. We believe that quartz can be a powerful source of inspiration and we are here to bring your vision to life.
